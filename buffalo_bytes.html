Buffalo Bytes

Buffalo Bytes are a small, highly mobile robot platform.
Each Buffalo Byte is capable of locomotion, control,
and communication and new functionalities can be easily integrated.

The Buffalo Byte was filmed outside of Furnas Hall and used the on-board IMU to drive in straight lines.

Publications

Singh, S., Truax, R., and St. Pierre, R. Buffalo Byte: A Highly Mobile and Autonomous Millirobot Platform. IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters, 2024.
